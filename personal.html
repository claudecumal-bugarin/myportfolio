Hi, I’m Claude (skip the “John”—it’s not nakaka-slay for me). I turned 16 last June 17 and I’m a Grade 11 TVL-ICT Programming student. My life? A mix of coding drills, Mobile Legends battles, and shower karaoke sessions—I’m a Top Philippines Kagura main, and proud of it! Back in Grade 9, I worked hard to fund my studies by ranking up ML accounts for clients. Gaming wasn’t just fun; it helped me survive tough times.

My biggest flex? Graduating JHS with honors (91% average) while juggling ML gigs. Piloting taught me discipline—imagine grinding strangers’ accounts and acing exams! Sure, I couldn’t keep that hustle forever, but it proved I adapt fast, like Kagura dodging ganks. Kagura’s not just my main; she’s my metaphor: “Map pressure? Life pressure? Same focus.” Now, I code instead of chasing Mythic ranks, but the grind stays—whether fixing loops or singing Taylor Swift off-key in the bathroom.

Why ICT Programming? I want to build systems (or even games) that create opportunities for others. ML showed me tech’s power to connect people—now I want to be that bridge. Every code loop feels like a Kagura combo: sharp and full of purpose. My mantra? “Lag doesn’t last; keep pushing.” And when coding gets stressful? I blast OPM songs even if I’m out of tune—walang accuracy, basta enjoy lang!

So yeah, I’m Claude: gamer, coder, shower singer, all grit. What’s next after this respawn? Let’s see. 💻🎤
